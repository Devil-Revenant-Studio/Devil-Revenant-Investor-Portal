## 🎮 The Game Experience

### World Design (4 Interconnected Realms)

Players can **switch between worlds** at any time, carrying their inventory and progression:

**1. Angel Realm** (Light)
- Sacred territories with celestial architecture
- Holy magic system with healing/protection spells
- Celestial NPCs with righteous quests
- Safe zones for new players
- **Economic advantage:** Lower transaction fees on DOT marketplace

**2. Demon Realm** (Dark)
- Infernal landscapes with corruption mechanics
- Dark magic focusing on power/control spells
- Demonic NPCs with morally gray storylines
- PvP-enabled zones with higher rewards
- **Economic advantage:** Higher DOT drop rates

**3. Human Realm** (Balanced)
- Mortal plane with traditional RPG zones
- Balanced magic system
- Human NPCs with political intrigue quests
- Great for economy grinding
- **Economic advantage:** Stable resource nodes

**4. Hybrid Realm** (Experimental)
- Liminal space merging all three realms
- Mixed gameplay mechanics
- Hybrid NPCs with unique knowledge
- Endgame content
- **Economic advantage:** Rare NFT drops, limited-time events

### Cross-World Mechanics
- **NPC Knowledge:** NPCs aware of events in other worlds
- **Event Broadcasts:** Boss kills, wars, market crashes affect all realms
- **Player Economy:** Arbitrage opportunities between world markets
- **Narrative Continuity:** Single cohesive lore spanning 4 worlds
