## Anti-Cheat & Fair Play

### 1. Anti-Cheat Detection

**Detection Methods:**
- Server-side behavior analysis (detecting impossible stats)
- Client-side integrity checks (modified game files)
- Pattern recognition (sudden skill jumps, impossible reaction times)
- Player reports + manual review

**Automated Bans:**
- Obvious aimbots/wallhacks → immediate permanent ban
- Exploits → account rollback + temporary suspension
- RMT/Account selling → permanent ban + associated accounts flagged

### 2. Appeal Process

**Ban Appeal:**
1. Player submits appeal within 30 days
2. Manual human review (3–5 business days)
3. Evidence presented (game logs, video, chat)
4. Final decision communicated via email

**Successful Appeals:**
- Account reinstatement
- Compensation if wrongfully banned (cosmetics, premium time)

### 3. Fair Play Policies

**Leaderboards:**
- Cheaters automatically removed
- Leaderboard history cleaned retroactively
- Seasonal resets (monthly, quarterly)

**Competitive PvP:**
- Ranked matches use server-authoritative combat (no client-side damage)
- ELO rating system (1000–2800 rating range)
- Proof-of-Play requirement (10 min. matches before ranked eligibility)
